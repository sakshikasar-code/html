Santorini, Greece

Overview

Santorini is a picturesque Greek island in the Aegean Sea, known for its stunning sunsets, whitewashed houses, and blue-domed churches.

The island is located in the Cyclades group of islands and is a popular destination for honeymooners and travelers seeking a romantic getaway.

Must-see Attractions

  • Oia Village: A charming village with stunning views of the caldera and the sea.
  • Fira: The capital town of Santorini, with a rich history and stunning architecture.
  • Akrotiri: A ancient Minoan city, with well-preserved ruins and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Getting There

The nearest airport is Santorini Airport (JTR), which is located about 6 km from Fira.

Visitors can also arrive by ferry from Athens or other nearby islands.

Accommodations

Santorini offers a wide range of accommodations, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly vacation rentals.

Some popular options include:

  • Hotel Mama's Home: A family-run hotel with stunning views of the caldera.
  • Andronis Luxury Suites: A luxurious resort with private pools and stunning views.
